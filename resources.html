This site is prep material — you still have to sit the real exam with an EPA-approved proctor. Here are the common ones, plus official references and the handful of numbers and code sections worth bookmarking.

Common approved testing organizations

ESCO Institute

Industry-standard 608 exam used by most community colleges and trade schools. Can be taken online (Core + Type I) or proctored.

escogroup.org

Mainstream Engineering

Offers free online EPA 608 certification for Type I and proctored options for Type II/III.

epatest.com

RSES

Refrigeration Service Engineers Society administers 608 through a network of chapters.

rses.org

HVAC Excellence

Full 608 proctored testing plus a wide menu of HVAC competency exams.

hvacexcellence.org

Useful references

  • ASHRAE Standard 34 — refrigerant classification and safety group letters.
  • AHRI Standard 700 — purity spec for reclaimed refrigerant.
  • ARI 740 — performance spec for recovery/recycling equipment.
  • 49 CFR 173 — DOT hazardous-materials shipping rules for cylinders.
  • 40 CFR Part 82 Subpart F — the regulatory text of Section 608.
